Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What should I bring to my spa appointment?

+

Just bring yourself and relax! We provide all necessary amenities including robes, slippers, towels, and toiletries. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork and begin your relaxation journey.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

+

We recommend arriving 15-20 minutes before your scheduled appointment. This allows time to check in, change into spa attire, and settle into a state of relaxation before your treatment begins.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.

Are your products organic and cruelty-free?

+

Yes! We use only premium organic and cruelty-free products from trusted brands. All our products are carefully selected to ensure they are safe, effective, and environmentally responsible.

Do you offer couple's spa packages?

+

Absolutely! We offer romantic couple's packages that include side-by-side massage treatments in a private room, followed by access to our relaxation lounge. Perfect for anniversaries, special occasions, or simply quality time together.

Can I purchase gift certificates?

+

Yes! Gift certificates are available for any amount or specific treatment. They make perfect gifts for birthdays, holidays, or any special occasion. Contact us to purchase.
