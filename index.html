About Star and Sky Spa

Star and Sky Spa is a trusted and professional spa located in Katpadi, Vellore, offering high-quality wellness and relaxation therapies. With over 10 years of experience, we are known as one of the most reliable and professional spas in Vellore, providing services delivered by well-trained and certified therapists in a clean, calm, and welcoming environment. Our commitment to hygiene, customer care, and personalized treatments has earned us the trust of clients across Katpadi and the entire Vellore region, making Star and Sky Spa a preferred destination for premium spa experiences.
